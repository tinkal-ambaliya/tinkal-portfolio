Capital Valley Marketing
Website System

Complete WordPress-based marketing automation system with AI integration

Course: MIS 160/161 Team Size: 5 members Duration: 12 weeks Year: 2025

Project Overview

The Challenge

Capital Valley Marketing, a Sacramento-based B2B marketing consultancy, faced significant operational inefficiencies with their Squarespace website. Manual data entry consumed 260+ hours annually, poor SEO performance resulted in $8,000-$12,000 in lost clients, and the platform couldn't integrate essential tools like CRM or AI chatbots.

The Solution

Our team designed and prototyped an integrated WordPress ecosystem featuring Elementor Pro for easy content management, Vendasta CRM for automated lead capture, AI chatbot for 24/7 customer engagement, and advanced SEO tools to improve search visibility and organic traffic.

The Impact

The recommended system would save $20,000-$27,000 annually in operational costs, eliminate 260 hours of manual data entry, achieve 200-300% increase in organic traffic, and provide 60% inquiry resolution through AI automation with sub-30-second response times.
